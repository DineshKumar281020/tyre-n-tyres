Privacy Policy

At Tyre N Tyres Store, we value your privacy and are committed to protecting the information you share with us. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your data when you interact with us.

Information We Collect

We may collect personal information such as your name, phone number, email address, and any details you provide when you contact us or inquire about our products and services. We also gather non-personal data, such as your IP address and website browsing patterns, to improve your experience.

How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to:

  • Provide you with the products and services you request.
  • Respond to your inquiries and offer customer support.
  • Inform you about updates, offers, and promotions (with your consent).
  • Analyze website usage to enhance our services and user experience.
Protecting Your Data

We implement secure methods to protect your personal information from unauthorized access, misuse, or disclosure. While we strive to keep your data safe, no system can guarantee complete security.

Sharing Your Information

We do not sell your personal information. Your data may be shared with trusted partners only when necessary to provide our services or comply with legal requirements.

Cookies Policy

Our website uses cookies to improve your browsing experience and analyze traffic. You can manage your cookie preferences through your browser settings.

Your Rights

You have the right to access, update, or delete your personal information. To exercise these rights or for any privacy-related concerns, please contact us.

Updates to This Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. Any changes will be posted on this page, and we encourage you to review it regularly.
